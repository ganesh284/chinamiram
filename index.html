    About Panchayti

    Chinamiram Gram Panchayat is the local self-government of the village. The elected members of the gram panchayat are headed by a Sarpanch. The current Sarpanch of the village, Srinivasa Raju, was awarded the Nirmala Grama Puraskaram for the year 2013. The efforts put forth by the local community and administration have yielded outstanding results. Chinamiram village, located in Bhimavaram Mandal of West Godavari district, has been declared a "Smart Village" in the country.

    • Drainage System Maintenance

      The Panchayat has introduced several measures to maintain the drainage system, ensuring a cleaner and healthier environment for the villagers.

    • Drinking Water Supply

      Efforts have been made to improve the drinking water supply, ensuring clean and safe water for the residents of Chinamiram.

    • Road Network Development

      The village has worked on road network development, improving connectivity and infrastructure for better accessibility.

    • Increased Taxes for Infrastructure

      To meet the financial needs and strengthen village infrastructure, the Panchayat introduced increased taxes, generating Rs 1 crore in revenue.

    • Welfare, Development, and Health

      The village has adopted welfare, development, and health as the main parameters for the overall development, setting an example for many habitations in Andhra Pradesh.
